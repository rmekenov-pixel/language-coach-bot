Privacy Policy — Language Coach Bot

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Language Coach Bot ("we", "our", "the bot") is a WhatsApp-based English language learning assistant. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with Language Coach Bot via WhatsApp, we collect:

  • Phone number — used to identify your account and deliver responses
  • Message content — your text messages to the bot, stored to maintain conversation context
  • Learning level — your English proficiency level (A1, A2, B1), updated based on your progress

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide personalised English language coaching
  • Maintain conversation history to improve learning continuity
  • Track your learning progress and recommend appropriate materials

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Language Coach Bot uses the following third-party services:

  • WhatsApp Business API (Meta) — to send and receive messages. Subject to WhatsApp Privacy Policy
  • Groq API — to generate AI responses. Messages are processed by Groq's language models
  • Railway — cloud hosting for the bot infrastructure

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (US West region). We implement standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access.

6. Data Retention

We retain your conversation history to provide continuity in your learning experience. You can delete your conversation history at any time by sending /reset to the bot.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Clear your conversation history using the /reset command
  • Stop using the service at any time

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: rmekenov@gmail.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via WhatsApp message.
